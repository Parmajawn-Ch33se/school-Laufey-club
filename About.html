What is the Laufey Club?

The Laufey club is a club created by Justin Mabilog, about singer/song-writer, grammy nominated and winner, Laufey Lín Bing Jónsdóttir. Laufey fans who are apart of the Connecting Waters school meet up once a week (not yet established) to discuss her music, recent events, and 'Laufeycore' related things.

What do we do?

Each week, we talk about 1-2 songs from her albums, ep, or we talk about a single and dive into the lyrics, and what it means. We also chat about recent Laufey/Laufey-related news, events, and/or posts!
