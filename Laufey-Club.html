Who is Laufey?

Laufey, (pron. lay-vay) whose full name is Laufey Lín Bing Jónsdóttir, is a talented Icelandic-Chinese singer-songwriter and musician. Born on April 23, 1999, in Reykjavík, Iceland, she has gained significant recognition in the music industry, particularly in the early 2020s. Laufey is celebrated for her unique blend of jazz-inspired pop, which showcases her smooth vocals and intricate songwriting.

Her music often features a mix of contemporary pop elements with classic jazz influences, creating a sound that resonates with a wide audience. Laufey's rise to fame has been marked by her engaging performances and heartfelt lyrics, making her a standout artist in today's music scene.

In addition to her musical achievements, Laufey has recently made headlines with her concert film, "A Night At The Symphony: Hollywood Bowl," which premiered on December 6, 2024. This film highlights her artistry and connection with fans, further solidifying her place in the music world.

Overall, Laufey is an artist to watch, with a promising career ahead and a growing fanbase that appreciates her distinctive style and musical talent.
